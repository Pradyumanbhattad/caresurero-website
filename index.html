FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about RO purification.

What is good water quality vs bad water quality?+
Good drinking water is clear, odourless, and has a TDS (Total Dissolved Solids) level between 50–150 ppm. Water above 300 ppm can taste bitter or salty, and above 500 ppm is generally not recommended for daily drinking. Regardless of your source, an RO purifier ensures your family always gets water at the ideal quality level.
Do you offer free water testing?+
Yes! We offer a free TDS test and water quality assessment at your home. Our technician visits, tests your water on the spot, and recommends the right purifier — all at zero cost and zero obligation to purchase.
How quickly can you install?+
We offer same-day installation across Hyderabad in most cases. Our trained technicians bring all tools and fittings. Installation takes just 1–2 hours. Call us before noon and we can usually be there the same day.

We cover all major areas — Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Kukatpally, Gachibowli, HITEC City, Kondapur, Begumpet, Secunderabad, Ameerpet, Dilsukhnagar, Uppal, Miyapur, Manikonda, Nizampet and surrounding areas.
What is an AMC and do I need one?+
AMC stands for Annual Maintenance Contract. It covers regular filter changes, servicing and repairs at a fixed annual cost — much cheaper than paying per visit. We strongly recommend an AMC to keep your purifier performing at its best year-round.
How often do filter changes depend on water quality?+
Filter change frequency depends on your water quality and usage. In areas with higher TDS or sediment, filters may need changing every 4–6 months. In areas with cleaner source water, filters can last 6–12 months. Our AMC plan monitors this for you automatically.
